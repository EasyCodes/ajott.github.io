Hazards of Travel

Sunburn

Sunburn Effects
One Level -1D on all Awareness tests
Two Levels As above, and -1D on Fighting and Marksmanship tests and any test involving concentration for 1d6 days. Permanent -1D on Persuasion tests.
Three Levels As above, and permanent -1D on Endurance tests

Frostbite

Frostbite Effects
One Level -1D on all Awareness tests
Two Levels As above, and -1D on Fighting and Marksmanship tests and any test involving manual dexterity
Three Levels As above, and -1D on Deception and Persuasion tests
