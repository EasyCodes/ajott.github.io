Vera is a new-ish take on Space Invaders. You have a limited amount of ammunition per level (don't worry, your shots are refunded if you hit a target), and the enemies speed up the more levels you get through.


Move with your mouse, and click to shoot.


Periodically, powerups will spawn.

  • A: Gives you +3 ammo
  • I: Gives you infinite ammo for a short time
  • M: You fire 3 shots at a time, in a spread
  • B: Your bullets are bigger
Powerups are not carried over between levels.
